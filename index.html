About Anton

At Anton's, we take pride in being more than just a restaurant; we are a warm and welcoming neighborhood gathering place. Our passion for creating delectable dishes and fostering a sense of community has transformed us into a beloved local haven. Whether you choose to make a reservation through Resy or walk in spontaneously, we promise an unforgettable experience that combines culinary mastery with the comfort of home-cooked goodness. Join us at Anton's, where we celebrate the joy of dining together and cherish the connections made around our tables – because, at Anton's, we're not just serving meals, we're creating lasting memories as part of our extended family.

Our menu

Our menu at Anton's is a carefully curated symphony of flavors, showcasing the best of locally-sourced ingredients and seasonal produce. We take pride in crafting a diverse selection of dishes, ranging from time-honored classics to innovative culinary creations, designed to delight your taste buds and leave you craving more. Our talented chefs artfully combine traditional techniques with modern flair, ensuring every plate is as visually stunning as it is delicious. At Anton's, we cater to various dietary preferences, offering vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options, so everyone can enjoy a memorable dining experience. Embark on a culinary adventure with us and discover the exquisite tastes that make Anton's the go-to destination for food lovers in the neighborhood.
